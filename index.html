2. Data We Collect

GFIT may collect the following user information through secure and encrypted means:

  • Personal identification data: name, email address, gender, date of birth.
  • Health and activity data: fitness goals, weight, workout and nutrition logs.
  • Device data: device model, OS version, and app version for performance analytics.
  • Future Integration: payment information (credit/debit card) processed exclusively through secure, compliant third-party gateways in alignment with Google Play Billing Policy and PCI-DSS standards.

3. How We Use the Data

  • To provide intelligent and personalized fitness recommendations.
  • To synchronize workout progress and activity history across sessions and devices.
  • To send optional health and wellness reminders (only if users opt in).
  • To enhance performance, identify issues, and ensure a seamless user experience.
  • To support future secure payment features for premium services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information may be shared only with vetted partners necessary for service functionality:

  • Cloud storage providers — for secure data backup and availability.
  • Analytics platforms — for aggregated and anonymized usage insights.
  • Payment gateways — for transaction processing under strict compliance protocols.

All third-party partners adhere to Google Play Data Safety, GDPR, and global privacy standards.

5. Data Security

GFIT employs industry-leading encryption (AES‑256) and access control protocols to protect all health and personal data. Our infrastructure is routinely audited for compliance, and access is granted only on a need‑to‑know basis under secure authentication measures.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide essential app functionality and comply with applicable legal requirements. Users may delete their account or request full data erasure at any time by contacting contactus@gdinexus.com. Requests are handled promptly and transparently.

7. Children’s Privacy

GFIT is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided personal information, they should contact us immediately for swift removal of the data.
