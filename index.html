Privacy Policy for GFIT

Effective: October 22, 2025  ·  GDI Nexus — GFIT (for Ben Stamina Factory)

1. About Us

GDI Nexus ("we", "our", "us") is a software development company committed to building secure, privacy-respecting fitness and health applications.

Company: GDI Nexus
Developer Contact: contactus@gdinexus.com

2. Data We Collect

GFIT may collect the following user data through secure means:

  • Personal identification data: name, email address, gender, date of birth.
  • Health and activity data: fitness goals, weight, workout and nutrition logs.
  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version for performance analytics.
  • Future Integration (2026): payment information (credit/debit card) — processed by secure third-party gateways in compliance with Google Play Billing Policy and PCI-DSS.

3. How We Use the Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized fitness recommendations and tailored user experiences.
  • To synchronize workout progress and statistics across sessions and devices.
  • To send health reminders and notifications, only if the user opts in.
  • To improve app performance, diagnose issues, and fix bugs.
  • To enable payments in future releases (processed by compliant payment providers).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data may be shared only with trusted third parties when necessary to provide or improve our services:

  • Cloud storage providers (for encrypted data backup).
  • Analytics services (aggregated and anonymized usage analysis).
  • Payment processors (for future transactions).

All third parties engaged by GDI Nexus are contractually obligated to comply with applicable privacy requirements including Google Play's Data Safety policies and GDPR where applicable.

5. Data Security

We protect user information using commercially reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards. Fitness and health data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256. Access to production systems is restricted by access control and secure authentication.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide core app functionalities and to comply with legal obligations. Users may delete their account or request complete data deletion at any time by contacting contactus@gdinexus.com. We will respond to deletion requests in accordance with applicable law.

7. Children’s Privacy

GFIT is not targeted to children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to GFIT, contact us immediately at contactus@gdinexus.com so we can remove the information.

8. Future Updates

Our privacy practices may evolve as the app develops new features such as payments and AI-driven insights. Updated versions of this policy will be posted at:
